About Univolt Power

Building a cleaner, more sustainable future through innovation and engineering excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Univolt Power began with a simple mission: to make solar energy accessible, reliable, and affordable for everyone. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a leading EPC provider in the region.

Over the past decade, we have successfully delivered over 500MW of solar projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner in the renewable energy transition.

We believe that the future of energy is distributed, digital, and decarbonized. Every project we undertake brings us one step closer to that reality.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, cost-effective, and sustainable solar energy systems that eliminate power instability and reduce energy costs.

Our Vision

To become East Africa’s leading renewable energy solutions provider, transforming how energy is generated, stored, and consumed.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Safety, and Sustainability are at the core of everything we do.

Leading the way in sustainable energy solutions. We provide end-to-end solar engineering, procurement, and construction services.
